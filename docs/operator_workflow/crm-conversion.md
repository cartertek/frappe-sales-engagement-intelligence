# CRM Conversion

SEI prepares conversion context, but Frappe CRM owns the sales records. Conversion is explicit and manager-controlled. Lifecycle suggestions do not automatically mark prospects Ready for CRM Conversion; an operator must use the Mark Ready for CRM Conversion action.

1. Confirm the prospect is Qualified or Manually Approved.
2. Confirm it is not Rejected and not Do Not Contact.
3. Confirm no CRM Lead has already been created.
4. Mark Ready for CRM Conversion. If a requirement is not met, the form displays a checklist showing which checks passed or failed.
5. Preview CRM conversion and review duplicate candidates.
6. Create or link CRM Lead, CRM Organization, Contact, and CRM Deal only with explicit action.
7. Create a CRM Deal only where there is a commercial basis or manager override.
8. Sync SEI context to CRM after linking or creation where needed.

SEI never creates ERPNext Lead, Opportunity, Quotation, Customer, or other ERPNext commercial records.
