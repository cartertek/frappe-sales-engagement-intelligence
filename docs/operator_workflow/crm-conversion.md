# CRM Conversion

SEI prepares conversion context, but Frappe CRM owns the sales records. Conversion is explicit and manager-controlled.

1. Confirm the prospect is Qualified or Manually Approved.
2. Confirm it is not Rejected and not Do Not Contact.
3. Mark Ready for CRM Conversion.
4. Preview CRM conversion and review duplicate candidates.
5. Create or link CRM Lead, CRM Organization, Contact, and CRM Deal only with explicit action.
6. Create a CRM Deal only where there is a commercial basis or manager override.
7. Sync SEI context to CRM after linking or creation where needed.

SEI never creates ERPNext Lead, Opportunity, Quotation, Customer, or other ERPNext commercial records.
