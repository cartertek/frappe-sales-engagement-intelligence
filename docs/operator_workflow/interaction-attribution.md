# Interaction Attribution

SEI Interaction Attribution connects outreach outcomes back to prospect, signal, thesis, asset, offer, channel, and CRM records.

Create an attribution record after a manually reviewed outreach action or outcome. Use interaction type, channel, date, response category, and notes to capture what happened. Link CRM Lead or CRM Deal when the outreach has moved into Frappe CRM.

Attribution supports reporting and feedback. It does not send follow-ups, change lifecycle automatically, or mutate reports.
